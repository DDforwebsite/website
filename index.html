Our Story

A Legacy Built on Skilled Hands & Warm Care

For more than 25 years, Pooja Beauty Parlour has been a trusted name for brides, families, and everyday clients seeking beauty care that feels personal, precise, and unhurried.

Every service here carries the touch of real experience — the kind that only comes from decades of practice, listening closely to what each client wants, and refining technique visit after visit. From bridal transformations to a quick threading session between errands, the same care and attention goes into every chair.
